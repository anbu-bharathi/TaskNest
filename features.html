Mark Attendance

Mark your presence using mobile GPS. Easily track who is in, when, and where. This ensures accurate time logs and simplifies attendance tracking for remote and hybrid teams. GPS tracking enhances visibility and reduces manual errors, making workforce management seamless.

Leave Management

Submit and track leave requests with automatic manager notifications. Keep all leave records centralized and accessible. Automated workflows reduce approval delays and give real-time leave balances to employees.

Calendar View

Get a bird's-eye view of all team attendance, leaves, and shifts on a visual calendar. Helps HR and team leads to plan resources effectively and avoid scheduling conflicts. Filters and color codes make it easier to understand employee availability.

Multiple Shift

Set up various shift timings, assign team members and automate tracking. Customize shifts for different teams or departments, and automate overtime calculation. Ensures efficient staffing and reduces errors in manual shift tracking.

Geofencing

Restrict punch-in access to specific geographic office zones for better compliance. Prevents misuse of attendance system by ensuring location-specific attendance. Improves transparency and trust in the attendance process.

Workflow Management

Design, track, and manage workflows efficiently across teams.
